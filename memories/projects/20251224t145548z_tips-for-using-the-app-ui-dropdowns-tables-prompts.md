- Many dropdowns support multiple selections and creating new entries on the fly. Type part of a word and pick Create new entry to add items; press Enter to confirm.\n- Tab moves to the next field, not completing a selection.\n- Edit an item: place cursor after it, press Backspace to edit; click to the right of the item, backspace to edit the label, then press Tab to finish.\n- Delete items with keyboard: click after the last item, use the left arrow to go back, then Delete to remove items after the cursor.\n- Tables: checkboxes for multi-row selection; bulk action buttons; per-row action buttons; sorting by column headers; filtering; pagination; reordering columns.\n- Prompts and text windows: text history is saved across AI Coding, AI Answers, Map Vignettes, Soft Recode filters, and Auto Recode filter.\n- How to use text history: dropdown shows previous prompts with project name and date/time; use < and > to move through history; text area shows the selected prompt and lets you edit it; Expand to enlarge editor; Trigger to run and save to history.\n- Organization and notes: current project's prompts come first, then prompts from other accessible projects; duplicates are removed; your most recent prompt appears when you open each AI feature; lines starting with // in prompts are recorded in history but not sent to AI.\n- Source: content/999 Causal Map App/040 Tips for using the app ((tips-app)).md
