- Simplify any given text to a 6th-grade reading level (ages 11-12).
- Use short, simple sentences and common everyday words.
- Maintain the original key concepts while simplifying wording.
- Output should be just the simplified text, with no extra commentary.
- This technique improves readability and accessibility for a broader audience.
