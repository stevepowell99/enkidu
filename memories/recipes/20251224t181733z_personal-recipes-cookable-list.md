Summary

A concise, personal collection of reliably makeable recipes and shorthand notes for quick execution, meal planning and shopping.

Recipes & quick notes

- Moussaka: potatoes, lamb mince with passata and mixed spice, grilled aubergines, crème fraîche and grated cheddar; layer in a deep dish and bake at 220°C until cheese is golden.
- Chickpea & chorizo stew (Monika Linton): chorizo + pancetta + onion, splash red wine, sofrito + water, jars of chickpeas, garlic & oregano — quick, hearty, serve with bread.
- Anna Maria's lentil soup: fry onion in olive oil, add cumin (± turmeric), wash lentils ×3, add 1–2 L water + 2 chicken stock cubes, finish with lemon & pepper.
- Cheesy lentils / lentil pie: quick one-pan comfort dishes; shepherd-style topping for pie variant.
- Chili con carne, cabbage soup: simple, one-pot comfort meals with short ingredient lists.
- Potato pancakes (Kartoffelpuffer / reibedatschi): grate raw starchy potatoes + onion, add egg + flour, squeeze out liquid, fry until golden; serve sweet (applesauce) or savoury (creme fraiche).
- Krompiruša / Šica-style potato bakes, tortillas and pancakes: simple dough or layering techniques; keep recipes as shorthand formulas.
- Breads & doughs: BBC sourdough (starter, strong white flour, water, salt) and Jamie Oliver pizza dough (white / Tipo '00 flour, dried yeast, sugar, olive oil); tips: proving time, semolina for crisp base.
- Baking & festive: Mary Berry banoffee pie (digestive base, condensed milk caramel, bananas, double cream), Christmas cake (fruit soak, long bake and periodic feeding), royal icing options.
