- AI interviewer 'Harvest Assistant' tested within Outcome Harvesting (OH) to scale democratic evaluation.
- Pilot with KLAR! climate adaptation in Austria: 19 responses, 38 Outcome Statements; 6 SMART
- Benefits: scalability, inclusivity, transparency; real-time summaries; cost-effective data collection at scale
- Risks: disinformation, bias, exclusion; need guardrails and human oversight for ethics and sensemaking
- Proposed hybrid model: AI for scale + humans for evaluative judgment, dialogue, and equity
- OH is actor-centered, iterative, co-creates categories and interpretations with stakeholders; rigorous yet participatory
